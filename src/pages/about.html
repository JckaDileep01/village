About Our Village

About Village

Kareempur is a traditional rural village located near Vikarabad city, under Kotepally Mandal, Ranga Reddy District, Telangana. The village reflects the true spirit of rural Telangana, where agriculture, culture, and community life are deeply connected.

Despite being close to an urban center, Kareempur has preserved its peaceful village atmosphere. Farming remains the primary occupation, and the village lifestyle is shaped by seasonal cycles, local festivals, and shared responsibilities. Kareempur stands as a symbol of simplicity, resilience, and unity, while steadily aspiring for better infrastructure, education, and basic facilities.

History

Kareempur has a long-standing history rooted in agriculture and traditional settlement patterns common to the region. The village developed around fertile lands that supported farming families over generations. Elders of the village pass down stories of cooperation, shared labor, and collective growth.

Over time, Kareempur witnessed gradual changes—introduction of roads, electricity, schools, and access to nearby towns like Vikarabad. Though modernization touched the village slowly, the cultural identity, values, and traditions have remained strong.

Geography & Location

Kareempur is situated in Ranga Reddy District of Telangana, close to Vikarabad, making it accessible to nearby markets, educational institutions, and healthcare facilities.

The village is surrounded by agricultural fields and open countryside. The land is mostly flat to gently sloping, suitable for farming activities. The climate follows typical Telangana patterns with hot summers, monsoon rains that support agriculture, and mild winters.

Its location offers a balance between rural calm and urban connectivity, making Kareempur a favorable place for both living and farming.

Village Vision

The vision of Kareempur is to grow into a self-reliant, developed, and sustainable village without losing its cultural roots.

  • Improved drinking water and sanitation
  • Better education and healthcare facilities
  • Reliable electricity and road infrastructure
  • Support for farmers and local livelihoods
  • Encouraging youth participation and employment
  • Preserving traditions, unity, and environmental balance

Kareempur envisions a future where development reaches every household, opportunities are accessible to all, and the village stands as a model of balanced rural progress.
